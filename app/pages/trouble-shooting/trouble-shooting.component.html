Trouble Shooting

If you are facing any issues try to answer the following questions. It helps to solve the most problems. If not, post a comment to the Github gist (click on "Discussion").

  • Does your routing work using ng serve locally? If no, then your routing settings are wrong.
  • Does your web-server support htaccess files? Some web hoster like gitlab-pages does not support htaccess files.
  • Is the .htaccess file placed next to your index.html file?
  • Is your app installed in the root of your domain or in a subfolder?
  • What is your base-href value? If your app is located in a subfolder like https://example.com/awesome/ your base-ref attribute must be /awesome/. If that's not the case, the default value is /.
